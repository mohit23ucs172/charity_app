FAQ
Is my donation tax deductible? ▼
Yes. All donations are eligible for 80G tax deduction under the Income Tax Act. You will receive a certificate via email within 7 working days.
How is my money used? ▼
94% goes directly to field operations. 6% covers platform and operational costs. We publish quarterly impact reports on our website.
Can I cancel a recurring donation? ▼
Yes, you can cancel any time by contacting us on WhatsApp or email. No questions asked.
Is my payment information secure? ▼
All payments are processed via Razorpay with 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your card details.
